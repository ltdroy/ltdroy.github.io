Algorithmic art: 'Spiral Portal'

LTD / 09 May 2020
Categories: R ggplot2
Tags: Algorithmic Art

After enjoying Antonio Sánchez Chinchón’s DataCamp tutorial on using R and ggplot2 for algorithmic flower drawing based on Phyllotaxis, I decided to spend an afternoon exploring the techniques he covered. I extended the single flower techniques to a multi-flower image with each flower positioned to form an overall flower shape. The effect makes me think of a portal to a strange multicolor world.
